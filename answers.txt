Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git is useful for managing a team and allowing everyone to work on their own branches at once. When someone is ready to merge their branch, they are able to do so while ensuring they resolve all merge conflicts. It's also great for version control.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to many different fields. A common one is with finance, with analyzing stocks and other financial analysis.
